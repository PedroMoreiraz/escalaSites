### Business Model

What does the company sell?

### Positioning

How should the company be perceived?

What category does it belong to?

What category should it own?

---

### Audience

Who is the ideal customer?

Who is not the ideal customer?

What motivates purchasing decisions?

What creates resistance?

---

### Market

Who are the competitors?

What positioning opportunities exist?

What gaps exist in the market?

---

### Messaging

What should the website emphasize?

What should the website avoid?

What themes should appear repeatedly?

---

### Conversion

What action should visitors take?

What creates trust?

What creates hesitation?
