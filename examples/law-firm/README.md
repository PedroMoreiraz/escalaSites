# Examples - Law Firm

This folder contains reference projects for the legal sector.

## Purpose

Projects stored here serve as design and implementation references for new law firm clients.

Includes: corporate law, family law, criminal defense, labor law, real estate law.

## When to Use

- Before starting a new law firm project, review existing examples for pattern reference.
- Use examples to calibrate dials (Variance, Motion, Density) for the sector.
- Do not copy content from examples. Use them for structural and design reference only.

## Sector Notes

- Law firm projects require maximum credibility: credentials, bar associations, case outcomes.
- Tone is formal and authoritative. No casual language.
- Motion dial is typically low (1-3). Law firms communicate stability, not dynamism.
- Primary conversion: consultation booking or direct contact.
- Never fabricate case results or testimonials.

## Adding a New Example

After completing a law firm project, the EscalaSites team may promote it to this folder as a reference.

Only completed, QA-approved projects should be added as examples.
