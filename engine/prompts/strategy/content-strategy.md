# Generate Content Strategy

You are operating inside the EscalaSites ecosystem.

Read and obey:

* CLAUDE_MASTER_CONTEXT.md
* brand-profile.md
* page-architecture.md
* content-strategy-template.md

Your objective is to generate a complete content-strategy.md document.

---

## Purpose

Transform business strategy into communication strategy.

This document will become the source of truth for:

* Hero content
* Services content
* CTA content
* FAQ content
* About page content
* SEO content

The goal is to create a communication system, not isolated copy.

---

## Inputs

Use all available information from:

* Brand Profile
* Page Architecture
* Business Research
* Discovery Notes
* Firecrawl Research
* Existing Website Analysis

---

## Positioning Analysis

Determine:

### Core Positioning

How should the company be perceived?

### Market Position

How does the company differ from competitors?

### Strategic Opportunity

What positioning opportunity should the website exploit?

---

## Audience Analysis

Determine:

### Motivations

What motivates action?

### Frustrations

What creates pain?

### Objections

What prevents conversion?

### Desired Outcomes

What transformation is the audience seeking?

---

## Messaging Analysis

Determine:

### Core Message

The single most important idea visitors should remember.

### Supporting Messages

Messages that reinforce the core message.

### Messaging Pillars

Themes that should appear consistently throughout the website.

---

## Value Communication

Identify:

### Functional Benefits

Practical outcomes.

### Emotional Benefits

Emotional outcomes.

### Transformation Promise

The future state customers want to achieve.

---

## Trust Strategy

Determine:

### Trust Signals

Evidence that should be emphasized.

### Credibility Drivers

Elements that reduce perceived risk.

### Objection Handling

How trust should be built throughout the website.

---

## CTA Strategy

Determine:

### Primary CTA

The most important action.

### Secondary CTA

Supporting action.

### CTA Logic

Explain why these actions support the business goals.

---

## Tone of Voice

Define:

### Communication Style

How the brand should communicate.

### Vocabulary

Words and concepts that reinforce positioning.

### Avoided Vocabulary

Words, clichés, and phrases that weaken positioning.

Avoid:

* generic marketing language
* AI clichés
* exaggerated promises
* empty buzzwords

---

## Section Communication Strategy

Determine communication goals for:

### Hero

What must be communicated first?

---

### Services

How should services be framed?

---

### Testimonials

What proof should be reinforced?

---

### FAQ

Which objections should be eliminated?

---

### CTA

How should action be encouraged?

---

## SEO Content Strategy

Identify:

### Primary Topics

Topics aligned with commercial intent.

### Secondary Topics

Supporting authority topics.

### Search Intent

Explain the intent behind the target keywords.

---

## Content Rules

Determine:

### Always Emphasize

Messages that should appear frequently.

### Avoid

Messages that should never appear.
