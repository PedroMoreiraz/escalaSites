# Generate Page Architecture

You are operating inside the EscalaSites ecosystem.

Read and obey:

* CLAUDE_MASTER_CONTEXT.md
* brand-profile.md
* page-architecture-template.md

Your objective is to generate a complete page-architecture.md document.

---

## Primary Objective

Transform the Brand Profile into a production-ready website architecture.

The architecture must support:

* business goals
* user journey
* conversion goals
* SEO goals

---

## Architecture Principles

Every page must have a purpose.

Every section must have a purpose.

Do not add pages without justification.

Do not add sections without justification.

---

## User Journey Analysis

Determine:

* visitor intent
* visitor concerns
* visitor motivations
* trust requirements

Build the architecture around those findings.

---

## Conversion Analysis

Identify:

### Primary Conversion

The most important business action.

Examples:

* Schedule a consultation
* Request a quote
* Book a service
* Make contact

---

### Secondary Conversions

Supporting actions that move users toward the primary conversion.

---

## SEO Analysis

Determine:

* pages with highest search demand
* pages with highest commercial intent
* opportunities for service pages
* local SEO opportunities

---

## Page Planning Rules

Create the smallest architecture capable of achieving the business goals.

Avoid unnecessary pages.

Avoid SEO page inflation.

Avoid content duplication.

---

## Section Planning Rules

Each section must answer a specific user question.

Each section must contribute to:

* trust
* clarity
* conversion
* SEO

---

## Mobile Rules

Architecture must remain effective on mobile devices.

Prioritize:

* readability
* scanning
* navigation simplicity

---

## Design Direction

Carry over:

* Variance
* Motion
* Density

from the Brand Profile.

Adjust only if strongly justified.

---

## Output Requirements

The output MUST follow:

page-architecture-template.md

exactly.

Do not remove sections.

Do not change structure.

Populate all sections possible.

---

## EscalaSites Rules

Architecture should feel intentional.

Architecture should feel strategic.

Architecture should support premium positioning.

Avoid:

* generic landing page structures
* unnecessary sections
* bloated navigation
* template thinking

The final architecture should feel custom built for the company.
