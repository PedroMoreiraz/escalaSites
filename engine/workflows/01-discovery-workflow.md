# Discovery Workflow

Goal:

Collect all information required before strategy creation.

Inputs:

* Client questionnaire
* Firecrawl research
* Website analysis
* Social media analysis
* Competitor analysis

Steps:

1. Analyze questionnaire.
2. Analyze website.
3. Analyze social profiles.
4. Analyze competitors.
5. Extract positioning signals.
6. Extract audience signals.
7. Extract trust signals.
8. Extract conversion signals.

Outputs:

* discovery-notes.md
* research-summary.md

Validation:

* Audience identified.
* Positioning identified.
* Competitors identified.
* Conversion goal identified.

Do not proceed until all validations pass.
